# PRODUCT_SCOPE.md

## Product

Health Coach is a mobile AI health coach for users who want clearer daily guidance based on personal health data, lifestyle context, nutrition habits, symptoms, supplements, and progress feedback.

## Main Promise

Health Coach turns complex inputs into a simple daily answer:

```text
What should I do today to improve my state safely and realistically?
```

## Primary Outcomes

The product optimizes for:

- energy;
- mood and emotional stability;
- motivation;
- productivity;
- sleep quality;
- recovery;
- nutritional consistency;
- supplement adherence;
- understanding of personal health data.

## Primary Users

The first target users are adults with high cognitive load who want practical guidance instead of fragmented information.

Typical users may include:

- founders;
- managers;
- software engineers;
- designers;
- analysts;
- marketers;
- consultants;
- students with demanding schedules;
- knowledge workers who track health data.

## Active Product Areas

### 1. Today

Daily plan, priorities, core scores, tasks, progress, and AI insight.

### 2. Supplements

Personal supplement stack, schedule, catalog, safety notes, bee products, and adherence.

### 3. Body

Body systems, blood analyses, biomarker details, Braverman results, and AI health profile.

### 4. AI

Contextual assistant that explains recommendations and helps the user decide what to do next.

### 5. Profile

Personal data, goals, preferences, symptoms, lifestyle, subscription, settings, privacy, and progress history.

## Data Inputs

Health Coach can use:

- user profile;
- goals and preferences;
- symptoms;
- lifestyle questionnaire;
- nutrition description and constraints;
- blood analysis files or manual biomarker input;
- Braverman assessment;
- supplement routine;
- progress reviews;
- AI chat context.

## Core Outputs

Health Coach should produce:

- current state summary;
- health and system scores;
- limiting factors;
- prioritized recommendations;
- supplement stack;
- bee product suggestions when safe and relevant;
- nutrition guidance;
- weekly action plan;
- daily tasks;
- AI explanations;
- retesting or professional-evaluation prompts when appropriate.

## Monetization Scope

The primary monetization model is subscription access to the personal AI health coach.

Documented initial pricing may use Russian ruble examples, but pricing and payment-provider implementation should remain configurable.

Commerce, marketplace checkout, delivery, shipment, and affiliate purchase flows are not active product scope unless explicitly requested.

## Out of Scope

The current active product scope excludes:

- diagnosis;
- disease treatment claims;
- emergency medical triage;
- prescription drug recommendations;
- controlled substances;
- injectables;
- SARMs, peptides, or experimental compounds as user-facing supplement recommendations;
- clinic booking;
- supplement checkout and delivery;
- required delivery address collection;
- full electronic medical record functionality;
- clinician portal;
- wearable integrations as required functionality.

## Success Criteria

The product is successful when a user can:

1. understand their current state;
2. see what likely limits their energy, mood, motivation, productivity, sleep, or recovery;
3. receive a realistic plan for the week;
4. know exactly what to do today;
5. follow supplement and nutrition guidance safely;
6. ask AI for clarification;
7. review progress and receive adjusted guidance;
8. avoid information overload.
