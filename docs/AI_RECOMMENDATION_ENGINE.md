# AI_RECOMMENDATION_ENGINE.md

## Purpose

The purpose of the AI Recommendation Engine is to improve the user's overall state by continuously analyzing biological, psychological, behavioral, nutritional, and subjective health data.

The system is not designed to diagnose diseases.

The system is designed to improve:

- Emotional well-being
- Energy levels
- Motivation
- Productivity
- Recovery capacity
- Long-term health status

---

# Core Philosophy

Health Coach is not a blood test interpretation application.

Health Coach is an AI-powered health optimization system.

Laboratory results, psychological assessments, symptoms, lifestyle factors, nutrition, supplement data, bee product recommendations, and user feedback are combined into a single decision-making framework.

The objective is not only to optimize biomarkers.

The objective is to improve the user's real-life condition.

---

# Primary Success Metrics

The AI continuously attempts to improve:

1. Emotional State
2. Energy Levels
3. Motivation
4. Productivity

All four metrics are considered important and interconnected.

No single metric should be optimized at the expense of another.

---

# Data Sources

## Priority Level 1 — Laboratory Results

Blood biomarkers are considered the most objective source of information.

Examples:

- Hormones
- Vitamins
- Minerals
- Metabolic markers
- Inflammatory markers

## Priority Level 2 — User Symptoms

Examples:

- Fatigue
- Brain fog
- Anxiety
- Low mood
- Low motivation
- Poor concentration
- Sleep problems

Symptoms can override normal laboratory results if severe and persistent.

## Priority Level 3 — Braverman Assessment

The Braverman profile is used to identify neurotransmitter tendencies and behavioral patterns.

The Braverman Assessment does not replace laboratory data.

It supplements laboratory interpretation.

## Priority Level 4 — Historical Progress

The AI should evaluate trends over time.

Examples:

- Improving energy
- Worsening sleep
- Stable motivation
- Improved biomarkers

Progress is more important than isolated measurements.

# Recommendation Categories

The AI may generate recommendations in all available categories simultaneously.

Categories include:

- Sleep optimization
- Circadian rhythm correction
- Nutrition
- Physical activity
- Walking protocols
- Recovery protocols
- Supplement recommendations
- Bee product recommendations
- Mental practices
- Stress management
- Lifestyle adjustments
- Retesting recommendations

No recommendation category has automatic priority over another.

Recommendations are selected according to individual needs.

---

# Nutrition Agent

A dedicated AI Nutrition Agent should exist inside the application.

Responsibilities:

- Analyze eating habits
- Suggest dietary improvements
- Generate meal recommendations
- Modify existing meal plans
- Support recovery and energy optimization

Core principles:

- Favor minimally processed foods
- Favor foods close to their natural state
- Reduce or eliminate refined sugar
- Support nutrient density
- Consider user goals and health status

Future versions may include complete meal planning.

---

# Adaptive Feedback System

Every 14 days the AI initiates a health review.

Questions include:

- How is your energy?
- How is your motivation?
- How is your mood?
- How is your sleep?
- Have symptoms improved?
- Have symptoms worsened?

The AI adjusts future recommendations based on user feedback.

---

# Personal Action Plans

The AI generates actionable plans.

Default format:

7-Day Action Plan

Includes:

- Daily priorities
- Supplement schedule
- Lifestyle recommendations
- Sleep recommendations
- Physical activity goals

The plan is updated whenever significant new information becomes available.

---

# Decision-Making Logic

The AI evaluates:

1. Laboratory results
2. Symptoms
3. Braverman profile
4. Historical trends

The AI must identify:

- Root causes
- Limiting factors
- High-impact interventions

The AI should answer:

1. Why does the user currently feel this way?
2. What is limiting improvement?
3. What should be improved first?
4. Which actions will create the greatest benefit?

---

# Medical Safety Layer

The application does not diagnose diseases.

The application does not replace licensed medical professionals.

Whenever supplements, protocols, or health interventions are recommended, the AI should include a reminder encouraging consultation with a qualified healthcare professional.

The system should escalate recommendations for professional medical evaluation whenever findings may indicate a potentially serious health concern.
