## Purpose

The Braverman Engine is responsible for evaluating neurotransmitter tendencies, behavioral patterns, cognitive style, stress response, motivation drivers, emotional regulation, and personality-based optimization strategies.

The Braverman Assessment is not used as a medical diagnostic tool.

Its purpose is to improve personalization and increase the effectiveness of recommendations generated by Health Coach.

---

# Role Inside Health Coach

Health Coach evaluates users through three major layers:

## Layer 1 — Biomarker Layer

Source:

- Blood analysis
- Laboratory biomarkers

Purpose:

Identify physiological limitations and deficiencies.

## Layer 2 — Neurotransmitter Layer

Source:

- Braverman Assessment

Purpose:

Identify behavioral tendencies, motivation patterns, stress responses, and cognitive preferences.

## Layer 3 — Subjective Layer

Source:

- Symptoms
- User feedback
- Progress reviews

Purpose:

Evaluate real-world experience and perceived well-being.

---

# Data Priority

Recommendation Priority:

1. Blood Analysis
2. User Symptoms
3. Braverman Assessment
4. Historical Progress

Braverman Assessment never overrides objective laboratory findings.

Instead, it provides context and explanation for how biological factors may influence behavior and emotional state.

# Neurotransmitter Attention-Area Layer

The AI may identify possible attention-area signals related to neurotransmitter patterns using:

- Braverman Assessment
- User symptoms
- Lifestyle factors
- Laboratory results

Potential attention categories are listed below. These labels are internal personalization signals. User-facing copy should use `attention area`, `pattern`, or `may be related to`, not definitive biological deficiency language.

## Dopamine Attention Area

Common indicators:

- Low motivation
- Fatigue
- Brain fog
- Procrastination
- Reduced ambition
- Poor focus

## Acetylcholine Attention Area

Common indicators:

- Poor memory
- Reduced learning ability
- Reduced creativity
- Cognitive slowing
- Difficulty concentrating

## GABA Attention Area

Common indicators:

- Anxiety
- Stress sensitivity
- Sleep problems
- Restlessness
- Excessive mental activity

## Serotonin Attention Area

Common indicators:

- Poor mood
- Low emotional stability
- Sleep issues
- Reduced enjoyment
- Increased emotional sensitivity

# Recommendation Personalization

The same recommendation may be presented differently depending on the archetype.

Example:

Goal:
Walk 8000 steps.

The Strategist:
Complete today's movement target.

The Creator:
Explore a new walking route today.

The Guardian:
Maintain your daily movement habit.

The Explorer:
Use today's walk as a recovery and enjoyment session.

# AI Interpretation Layer

The Braverman Engine helps answer:

1. What motivates this user?
2. What drains this user?
3. Which recommendations are most likely to be followed?
4. Which habits fit this user's personality?
5. How should the AI communicate with this user?

The objective is not only to improve health.

The objective is to maximize adherence to the protocol.

The best protocol is the protocol the user actually follows.

The neurotransmitter profile is relatively stable.

However, the AI continuously monitors:

- Motivation
- Emotional state
- Stress level
- Energy level
- Productivity
- Recovery quality

Review frequency:

Every 14 days.
