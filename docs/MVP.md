# MVP.md

## Project Name

Health Coach

---

# MVP Goal

Create the first working version of Health Coach that allows users to:

1. Explore the app before registration.
2. Purchase a subscription.
3. Create an account after payment.
4. Fill in personal and delivery information.
5. Upload blood test results.
6. Complete the Braverman Assessment.
7. Describe lifestyle and nutrition.
8. Receive AI-powered health analysis.
9. Receive personalized recommendations.
10. Receive supplement recommendations.
11. Receive bee product recommendations.
12. Receive a personalized 7-day action plan.
13. Track progress over time.

The MVP should demonstrate that Health Coach can improve:

- Energy
- Emotional state
- Motivation
- Productivity

through personalized recommendations.

# AI Safety Layer

The application:

- Does not diagnose diseases.
- Does not replace physicians.
- Provides educational and informational recommendations.

All supplement recommendations include a reminder to consult a qualified healthcare professional.

# MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if users:

- Explore the app before registration.
- Purchase subscription.
- Complete initial setup.
- Upload blood analyses.
- Complete the Braverman Assessment.
- Receive AI Summary.
- Follow Today’s Plan.
- Return every 14 days.
- Report improvements in energy, mood, motivation, or productivity.

The objective of MVP is validation, not full automation.
